LAW OF CHEMICAL COMBINATION

There are laws that govern the combination of simple substance to form complex substance. The simple substance are called REACTANTS while the comple substance are called PRODUCT. The laws of chemical combination are those laws that govern the changes from reactant to product involving teh mass, volume moles and their relationship. There are generally four basic laws of chemical combination which include the following;

  1. The laws of conservation of matter
  2. The laws of reciprocal proportion
  3. The laws of constant composition
  4. The laws of multiple proportion

THE LAWS OF CONSERVATION OF MATTER

This is also known as the law of conservation of mass. This laws state that matter is neither created nor destroyed in course of chemical reaction. This law simply means that the total mass of the product of a chemical reaction is equal to the total mass of the reaacting substance of a chemical reaction.
